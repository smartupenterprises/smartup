About Us

SmartUp Educational Institution( Now registered as Limited Liability Partnership named SmartUp Enterprises LLP ) has been revolutionizing the field of education substantially in Mattanchery (Kerala), the largest slum area in South India which is home to a young generation without even primary level education, mostly in the clutches of drugs. SmartUp Enterprises has been doing the lion’s share of work in molding 7863 children into socially responsible citizens by identifying their interests and providing them with the means to get access to education. SmartUp has been in the forefront of this movement by finding around 153 job opportunities for the young generation by focusing their talents to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.

Reiterating the fact that education is not a business, SmartUp believes in providing education as a service to make sure the children are elevated in both curricular and extracurricular through easy and simple learning. SmartUp focuses more on smart work as opposed to hard work using methods such as video classes, animation classes, smart classes, education games, and digital classes which has made SmartUp a household name among more than 7500 students.

Tutors at SmartUp who are also technical experts use state of the art technology as aids to teaching which assures the best quality education to the children. LCD projectors, latest learning and teaching equipment, an environment suitable for effective learning, online classes, online notes, and online exams also provide students with the best in class facilities. One to one online tests are conducted to assess the strengths and weaknesses of the students and the required training is provided to the students.

The tuition fee of a single student in Ernakulam, the commercial capital of Kerala, ranges around ₹1000 per subject. At the same time, SmartUp charges less than ₹90 for a subject. Economically backward students are taught for 25% lesser fees while destitute students are taught for free so that the students are molded into socially responsible citizens.

This initiative began with the aim to completely transition Mattancherry region through the hands of a teacher. From teaching initiatives to motivational classes, awareness classes, football tournaments, Independence Day celebrations, Republic Day celebrations, felicitation programs, academic tours, SmartUp has made sure that its efforts have a holistic impact on the students. These efforts in reviving the academic community was awarded by the Kerala Legislative Assembly speaker Shri. P Shriramakrishnan for the total contribution to the educational and cultural arena.
